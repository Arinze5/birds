Emu

AKA:Million dollar bird Scientific Name: Dromaius novaehollandiae Family: Dromaiidae Species: D. novaehollandiae Speed: 50 km/h Length: 180 cm Mass: 36 – 40 kg Wing Span: N/A Life Span: 12 – 20 years Location: Australia Diet: Seeds, flowers, berries, shoots and insects. It requires a large amount of water (9‐18) liters daily. It even eats stones, dirt and tin cans by accident.

Appearance:

Long neck, sharp bill, and small ears. It’s long feet have three toes. It’s double shaft feathers are light brown with dark tips.

Fact

  1. After the ostrich, it is the second-largest living bird by height.
  2. It is a flightless bird.
  3. It has small wings.
  4. It can defend himself with tremendously powerful kicks.
  5. It swallows gizzard stones which help to grind up food and accelerate digestion.
